## Why Kuantan Showed Resilience

### Possible Factors:

1. **Geographic Location**
   - Kuantan is the capital of Pahang, located on the east coast
   - May have better infrastructure/flood management than smaller cities
   - Urban planning may have better flood mitigation

2. **Operational Preparedness**
   - Better driver/merchant preparedness for monsoon season
   - Alternative delivery routes established
   - Stronger operational infrastructure

3. **Local Flood Impact**
   - Pahang was affected, but flooding may have been more localized
   - Kuantan city center may have been less affected than surrounding areas
   - Different flood pattern compared to Kelantan (Kota Bharu)

4. **Historical Resilience**
   - Kuantan has experienced monsoon seasons before
   - May have developed better coping mechanisms
   - Infrastructure improvements over time
