### 2.2 What is COPS?

**COPS (Completed Orders Per Session)** measures session efficiency:
- **Definition**: COPS = Completed Orders / Completed Sessions
- **Meaning**: Average number of completed orders per completed session
- **Efficiency Indicator**: Higher COPS = better session-to-order conversion

### 2.3 Key Insights

#### [A] COPS Improvement
- **COPS**: 1.06 → 1.07 (+0.9%)
- **Interpretation**: Each completed session now results in 1.07 completed orders (up from 1.06)
- **Efficiency Gain**: Slight improvement in session-to-order conversion

#### [B] Drivers of COPS Improvement
- **Completion Rate**: Improved from 90.0% → 92.5% (+2.5pp)
- **Impact**: Higher completion rate means more orders complete per session
- **Efficiency**: Better fulfillment quality contributes to COPS

#### [C] COPS vs Orders per Session
- **Orders per Session**: 1.13 (stable) - all orders
- **COPS**: 1.07 (improved) - completed orders only
- **Difference**: 0.06 gap represents incomplete orders per session
- **Improvement**: Gap reduced slightly due to better completion rate
