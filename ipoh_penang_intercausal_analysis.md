# Inter-Causal Analysis: Ipoh & Penang Growth Drivers
## Deep Dive Analysis for October 2025 vs September 2025

---

## Executive Summary

This analysis focuses specifically on **Ipoh** and **Penang** to identify what drives their growth from September to October 2025. While both cities show growth, their drivers differ significantly.

**Key Findings:**
- **Penang**: +5.4% order growth driven primarily by eater expansion (+8,040 eaters)
- **Ipoh**: +3.4% order growth driven by merchant expansion (+114 merchants) and operational efficiency
- **Penang** shows higher promotion penetration (56.4% vs 46.2%)
- **Ipoh** shows stronger merchant utilization despite lower growth rate

## 4. KEY INSIGHTS

### 4.1 Different Growth Models

**Penang - Demand-Pull Model:**
- Eater acquisition drives growth
- High promotion penetration (56.4%)
- Strong basket size (MYR 35.48)
- Higher order growth rate (5.4%)

**Ipoh - Supply-Push Model:**
- Merchant expansion drives growth
- Moderate promotion (46.2%)
- Lower basket size (MYR 31.05)
- Steady but lower growth rate (3.4%)

### 4.2 Strengths & Opportunities

**Penang Strengths:**
1. ✅ Strong eater acquisition capability
2. ✅ Effective promotion strategy (high penetration)
3. ✅ High basket size (premium segment)
4. ✅ High merchant utilization

**Penang Opportunities:**
1. ⚠️ Completion rate decline (-0.6pp) - needs monitoring
2. ⚠️ Basket size decline (-MYR 0.31) - maintain quality focus

**Ipoh Strengths:**
1. ✅ Strong merchant expansion
2. ✅ Stable operational efficiency
3. ✅ Balanced growth approach

**Ipoh Opportunities:**
1. ⚠️ Lower eater growth (1.3% vs Penang's 2.8%) - accelerate acquisition
2. ⚠️ Lower promo penetration (46.2%) - increase promotion investment
3. ⚠️ Lower basket size - focus on upselling
