# Local vs Foreign Eaters Analysis: Outer Cities Growth Drivers
## October 2025 vs September 2025

---

## Executive Summary

This analysis identifies whether the increase in eaters for Outer Cities (OC) is driven by **local Malaysians** or **foreign eaters**. The results show that **91.1% of eater growth comes from local Malaysians**, with only 8.9% from foreign eaters.

**Key Findings:**
- **Local Eaters**: +64,574 eaters (+4.0%) - **91.1% of total growth**
- **Foreign Eaters**: +6,288 eaters (+5.2%) - **8.9% of total growth**
- **Total Growth**: +70,862 eaters - primarily driven by local acquisition
