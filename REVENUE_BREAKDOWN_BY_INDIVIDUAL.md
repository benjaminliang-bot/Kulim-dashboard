---

## Revenue Source Definitions

### 1. Commission Revenue
- **Source**: `ocd_adw.f_food_metrics.commission_from_merchant`
- **Definition**: Gross billing revenue from commission on completed orders (deliveries and dine out)
- **Calculation**: Sum of `commission_from_merchant` for completed orders

### 2. Ads/Spotlight Revenue
- **Source**: `ocd_adw.agg_ads_merchant.accrued_amount_before_tax_local`
- **Definition**: Revenue Grab earns from ads/spotlight campaigns
- **Calculation**: Sum of `accrued_amount_before_tax_local` (Grab's revenue from ads)

### 3. MEX Funded Campaign
- **Source**: `ocd_adw.f_food_discount.total_mex_promo_spend`
- **Definition**: MEX-funded promotional campaign spending (hotdeals, delivery campaigns)
- **Calculation**: Sum of `total_mex_promo_spend` for MFP campaigns
