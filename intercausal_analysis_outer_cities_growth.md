# Inter-Causal Analysis: What Drives the Growth in Outer Cities (OC)
## Analysis of Factors Driving Increment from August/September to October 2025

---

## Executive Summary

This inter-causal analysis identifies the primary drivers behind the growth in outer cities (excluding Klang Valley) from August/September 2025 to October 2025. The analysis reveals **eater base expansion** as the #1 driver, supported by promotion investment, merchant expansion, and operational efficiency improvements.

**Key Growth Metrics:**
- **Orders**: +7.4% from September (544,421 more orders)
- **GMV**: +6.7% from September (MYR 17.2M increase)
- **Eaters**: +4.1% from September (70,862 new eaters)
- **Merchants**: +2.0% from September (971 new merchants)

---

## 1. PRIMARY GROWTH DRIVERS

## 7. Conclusion

The inter-causal analysis reveals that **eater base expansion** is the primary driver of growth in outer cities, contributing to a 7.4% increase in order volume and 6.7% increase in GMV from September to October 2025. This is supported by:

1. **Promotion Investment** (+11.2%) driving order attractiveness
2. **Merchant Expansion** (+2.0%) increasing supply capacity
3. **Operational Efficiency** improvements (completion + allocation rates)
4. **City-Level Performance** with Johor Bahru leading growth

The causal chain is clear: **New eaters → More orders → Better fulfillment → GMV growth**, with promotion investment and merchant expansion supporting this cycle.

**Strategic Focus**: Continue aggressive eater acquisition, maintain promotion investment, sustain merchant expansion, and focus resources on top-performing cities.
