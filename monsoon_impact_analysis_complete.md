## 10. Recommendations

### 10.1 Immediate Actions (Next Monsoon Season)

1. **Weather Monitoring Integration**
   - Integrate weather alerts into operations dashboard
   - Proactive communication to drivers/merchants during forecasted heavy rain
   - **ROI:** Prevent ~MYR 5.77M GMV loss per severe weather week

2. **Operational Adjustments**
   - Increase driver incentives during adverse weather (+20-30% surge pricing)
   - Extend delivery time SLAs during flood periods (add 15-20 min buffer)
   - Temporary merchant fee adjustments for affected areas (reduce commission 2-3pp)

3. **Customer Communication**
   - In-app notifications about potential delays
   - Transparent messaging about weather impact
   - Proactive order cancellation with full refund for severe weather

### 10.2 Medium-Term Actions (Next 3-6 Months)

1. **Resilience Building**
   - Identify flood-prone merchant locations (map historical flood zones)
   - **Priority cities:** Penang, Alor Setar, Ipoh (highest vulnerability)
   - Develop alternative delivery routes (avoid flood-prone areas)
   - Partner with weather services for early warning (3-5 day forecasts)
   - **Focus on northern/east coast cities:** Penang, Alor Setar, Kota Bharu, Kuantan, Sungai Petani, Kemaman

2. **Data-Driven Response**
   - Build predictive model for weather impact (ML model using historical data)
   - Pre-allocate resources based on weather forecasts (driver surge, merchant prep)
   - Dynamic pricing/incentives during forecasted disruptions (surge pricing 24-48h ahead)

3. **Merchant Support**
   - Flood-proofing support for high-risk merchants
   - Alternative preparation methods during forecasted disruptions
   - Supply chain backup plans

### 10.3 Long-Term Actions (6-12 Months)

1. **Infrastructure**
   - Work with local authorities on flood mitigation
   - Support merchant flood-proofing initiatives
   - Develop alternative delivery methods (boats, etc.) for flood-prone areas

2. **Insurance & Risk Management**
   - Weather disruption insurance for merchants
   - Driver safety protocols during adverse weather
   - Business continuity planning
