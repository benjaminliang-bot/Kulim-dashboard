## 7. Root Cause Analysis - Why Week 2 Was Critical

### Weather Pattern (Inferred from Data)

**Extended Period of Heavy Rain/Flooding:**
- **Oct 18-19:** Early signs (Kota Kinabalu, Ipoh, Penang, Kemaman <90% completion)
- **Oct 20:** Multi-city disruption (Penang, Ipoh, Kuantan, Sungai Petani)
- **Oct 22:** Continued impact (Penang 89.1%)

- **Oct 24:** Continued impact (Kota Bharu, Ipoh, Kemaman)
- **Oct 25:** Continued impact (Kemaman 88.9%)

**Pattern:** Extended period of heavy rain/flooding affecting northern/east coast regions over 7 consecutive days

### Operational Impact Chain

1. **Restaurant Closures:** Flooded premises unable to operate
   - Evidence: Unallocated orders, cancelled merchant orders
   
2. **Delivery Disruption:** Flooded roads, driver safety concerns
   - Evidence: Cancelled driver orders, order expirations
   
3. **Demand Suppression:** Customers not ordering during floods
   - Evidence: 40,565 sessions lost (-2.5%), 52,991 orders lost (-2.9%)

4. **Extended Duration:** 7 consecutive days of impact
   - Evidence: Multiple cities affected across Oct 20-26
