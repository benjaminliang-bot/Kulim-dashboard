# Local vs Foreign Eaters: Month-over-Month Comparison
## August, September, and October 2025 Analysis

---

## Executive Summary

This analysis compares local vs foreign eater growth across **August, September, and October 2025** to identify trends and patterns. The results show a **recovery pattern** where September saw a decline in foreign eaters (from August), followed by growth in October for both segments.

**Key Findings:**
- **Local Eaters**: Steady growth across all months (+2.0% Aug→Sep, +3.9% Sep→Oct)
- **Foreign Eaters**: Declined in September (-10.4% from August), recovered in October (+5.1% from September)
- **Overall Pattern**: Local-driven growth with foreign recovery in October
